# Assessments

###### Clinical & Psychological Evaluations

Sometimes it can be hard to understand why a child, teen, or adult is struggling at school, at work, or in everyday life. A psychological assessment helps us learn more about how someone thinks, learns, feels, and handles challenges. We look not only at the areas that feel difficult, but also at the person’s unique strengths. This helps us create clear recommendations and steps for support at home, school, or work.

**An assessment can answer questions like:**

- Why is my child having trouble with learning or paying attention?
- Could my teen's struggles be related to anxiety, mood, or behaviour?
- Does my child or teen need extra support at school, or might they benefit from enrichment opportunities?
- What supports would help me succeed in college, university, or the workplace?

**Some of the assessments we offer include:**

- **Psychoeducational Assessments** - to understand learning strengths and challenges, and to guide school planning.
- **Social, Emotional, and Behavioural Assessments** - to learn more about feelings, coping, and behaviour.
- **Autism Spectrum Disorder Assessments** - to explore whether someone may demonstrate aspects of or meet criteria for Autism Spectrum Disorder and identify helpful supports.
- **Gifted Assessments** - to determine if a child has advanced learning needs and how to nurture them.
- **College and University Assessments** - to support requests for accommodations and set students up for success in higher education.

Our goal is to give families and individuals a clear picture and a practical plan that makes life a little easier and helps people thrive.
