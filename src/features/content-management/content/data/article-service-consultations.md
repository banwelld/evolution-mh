Consultations are one-time or short-term sessions for people who want guidance around a specific concern. These meetings are focused on providing practical strategies, sharing information about helpful community resources, and exploring which services—whether with Evolution MHS or elsewhere—might be the best fit for your situation.

**Examples of consultations include:**

- A parent wanting support in responding to a child's behavior or difficulty.
- Guidance on collaborating with school staff to support a child's learning.
- Exploring how to best support a loved one who has experienced trauma or tragedy.
- Learning more about what therapy involves and whether it may be a good fit.
