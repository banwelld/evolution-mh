Evolving the Warmth

Exploring trauma-informed color strategies for Evolution MHS. Moving from heat to radiance—preserving the Phoenix spirit while prioritizing nervous system regulation.

3-Color Schemes
Regulatory Warmth

Morning Embers

Replaces "Aggressive Red" with Burnt Sienna and "Jarring Gold" with Dust Ochre. This scheme mimics the gentle warmth of a sunrise. It signals growth and optimism without the high-arousal intensity of fire.

Earth & Hearth

Anchors the warmth in the soil. Uses Deep Clay and Warm Sand. This is "Safe warmth." It feels architectural, rooted, and permanent—ideal for establishing a sense of safety for those in high distress.

Elegance & Center

The Inner Glow

Focuses purely on Soft Amber against an Obsidian contrast. It suggests a "Radiance" coming from within a safe, dark vessel. It is deeply sophisticated and minimizes sensory load by limiting the color story.

The Surprise Structural Pivot
Healing-First Architecture

The Veridian Phoenix

The Ethos: What if the Phoenix's evolution is into a life-giving force rather than a burning one? We replace the Red (biological signal for danger/blood) with Deep Veridian Teal (biological signal for water/safety/life). We keep the warmth of the original vision through a rich Glowing Copper accent. It is the most "Clinical" of the sets while remaining high-end.
